Harnessing Community Light to End Water Waste

Welcome to Aquanour, where water (Aqua) meets light (Nour). In a land where every drop is precious, our mission is to illuminate and eliminate water waste across Morocco by empowering our most valuable resource: our communities. We've built a simple, powerful system that transforms your phone into a tool for conservation. With just a single text message, you can instantly report a water leak, pinpointing the problem for a faster repair. Your alert doesn't just get logged; it helps safeguard the water that sustains our homes, our farms, and our future. Together, we can create a network of vigilance and turn a small action into a wave of positive change.

Use the navigation above to explore the different sections of our platform.

How It Works in 3 Simple Steps 1. SPOT THE LEAK See a broken pipe, a faulty public tap, or running water in the street? Take note of the location. 2. SEND AN SMS Text the location and a brief description of the leak to our national reporting number. It's fast, free, and works on any mobile phone. 3. SEE THE IMPACT Your report is instantly added to our live map, immediately alerting municipal technicians to the issue so they can act quickly.
